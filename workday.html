What is Workday Student ?

Traditionally, Workday is a cloud-based software that helps businesses manage HR and finances.

The platform also offers Workday Student which is a cloud-based student information system that offers a solution for managing student and faculty data, financial aid, and more. While, Workday for managing the busy and often complex processes within higher education sounds like a good idea, it’s execution is another story.


The Problem

Workday Student is a complex system that is not user-friendly. It is difficult to navigate and understand. This makes it hard for students and faculty to use the system effectively.
